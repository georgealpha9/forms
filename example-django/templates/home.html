Quick Start Guide

  1. Create a Form: Use the Form Builder to design your form with drag-and-drop
  2. Export JSON: Click "Export Form JSON" to download your form definition
  3. Import to Admin: Go to Django Admin and create a new Form Definition
  4. Paste JSON: Paste the exported JSON into the "Definition" field
  5. Save & Activate: Save the form and make sure "Is active" is checked
  6. View Form: Visit /renderer/your-form-id/ to see your live form
